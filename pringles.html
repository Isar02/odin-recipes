Pringles

Steps

STEP 1

Boil the potatoes as for mashed potatoes.

STEP 2

Drain all of the water, RUB them in mashed potatoes, adding butter, mushroom seasoning and spices (except paprika, we sprinkle it in the end ready-made chips). At the same time to breed a little bit of yeast with warm water and leave for 5 minutes.

STEP 3

Take cereal (without sugar!!). In a blender, grind into flour

Ingredients

  • potatoes (large -- 3 PCs
  • 30 grams butter
  • oat (flakes 100 g)
  • flour (how much dough -- 5 tbsp)
  • 1 egg
Description

Homemade Pringles potato chips
